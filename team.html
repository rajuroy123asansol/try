Our Team


Our team members are the heart and soul of our NGO, working tirelessly to make a difference in the world. Each individual brings their unique skills, experiences, and passion to the table, and together we form a dynamic group committed to achieving our goals. From organizing events to fundraising, from volunteering in the community to advocating for change, our team members go above and beyond to create positive impact. We are grateful for their dedication, hard work, and unwavering commitment to our mission. Our team truly embodies the spirit of compassion, collaboration, and service, and we are honored to work alongside them in pursuit of a better world.

Swamiji Cultural Trust, established in 2018, is an NGO dedicated to the development and improvement of the Asansol Area. Their focus areas include education, healthcare, and overall well-being of the community. Through their efforts, they aim to uplift lives, preserve cultural heritage, and foster unity among residents. Swamiji Cultural Trust strives to make a lasting impact and create a better future for Asansol.
